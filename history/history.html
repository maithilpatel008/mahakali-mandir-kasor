History

There is an ancient temple of the Goddess Mahakali located at the well-developed area of Charotar in village Kasor near Bhalej. It is 12 Kms away from Anand and 22 kms away from Dakor.

According to the beleif, a resident of the village Kasor, Kadubhai Govindbhai Patel dreamed of the Goddess Mahakali and Goddess told him that he will find a statue near the pond in the village. An idol of the Goddess Mahakali appeared when the eastern side of the pond in the village named was dug.

The idol of the Goddess was established on the pond side hill after that a temple was built by Kadubhai Govindbhai Patel in 1735 on the 8th day of Chaitra sud also known as Chaitri Sudi Atham. This temple is approximately 341 years old.

Navratri is celebrated with great enthusiasm in Chairta and Asho months. On both Navaratri, a majestic sacred fired also known as Havan is organized. More than 1000 coconuts also know as Shrifals are devoted in the same sacred fire.

Many beleivers of the Goddess Mahakali remain present on the day of Atham. When the beleivers of the Goddesss Mahakali worship with spiritual heart, The goddess fulfils all their wishes.
